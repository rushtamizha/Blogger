What is Branch?

Branch is an instant personal loan app that provides small-ticket loans to users without collateral. It evaluates loan eligibility using a combination of device data, bank statement analysis, and user-provided KYC. Branch partners with an RBI-registered NBFC to disburse loans in India.

Interest Rates & Hidden Costs — What to watch

Branch advertises fast loans but the **effective cost** depends heavily on processing fees and GST. The APR range is typically high (≈30–36%). Processing fees are often a percentage of the loan and may be deducted from the disbursed amount — meaning you get less than the face value.

Processing fee example

If processing fee = 12% + 18% GST on fee: for ₹10,000 loan, fee = ₹1,200, GST on fee ≈ ₹216, so total deductions ≈ ₹1,416 → you receive ≈ ₹8,584.

Late fees & penalties

Delayed payments attract late fees which vary by loan and can be a % of the outstanding. Always check the exact repayment schedule in-app.

Safety & Legality

Branch partners with an RBI-registered NBFC in India. KYC flows are standard (Aadhaar, PAN, bank verification). This makes Branch legally compliant — a big difference compared to many unregulated loan apps. However, always verify the lender details shown inside the app before accepting any loan.

Final Verdict

Branch is a legitimate, fast solution for short-term cash needs. But it is expensive. Use it selectively — for emergencies where you can repay on time. If you need affordable credit, explore personal loans from NBFCs/banks with longer tenures and lower APR.
