Final Voter List Check 2025 – How to Check Your Name Online

The Election Commission of India (ECI) releases the Final Voter List before every election. Citizens must verify their name to ensure they are eligible to vote. This guide explains how to check your name in the voter list online easily.

Why Checking the Final Voter List Is Important

  • Confirms your eligibility to vote
  • Ensures correct spelling of name and address
  • Avoids last-minute issues on election day
  • Helps download voter slip easily

How to Check Your Name in the Final Voter List

  1. Visit the official Election Commission voter portal
  2. Select Search by EPIC Number or Search by Personal Details
  3. Enter required details such as name, date of birth, or voter ID
  4. Submit the form to view your voter status
  5. Download or print your voter slip if available

Eligibility to Be Included in Voter List

  • Must be a citizen of India
  • Must be 18 years or older
  • Should be registered in the electoral roll
  • Must have valid address proof

Frequently Asked Questions

What if my name is missing from the final voter list?

You can apply for correction or inclusion through the ECI portal or contact your local Booth Level Officer (BLO).

Can I check voter list using mobile?

Yes, the official voter portal works on mobile phones and tablets.

Is checking voter list free?

Yes. The service is completely free and provided by the Government of India.
