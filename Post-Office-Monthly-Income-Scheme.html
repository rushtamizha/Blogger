5. Taxation

  • Interest is fully taxable
  • No TDS deducted by Post Office
  • No Section 80C benefits

6. Eligibility

  • Must be a Resident Indian
  • Must be 18+ years old
  • Minors (10+) can open account in their own name
  • Guardians can open for minors or dependents

8. How to Open an Account

  1. Visit your nearest Post Office
  2. Fill the POMIS account opening form
  3. Submit KYC documents (Aadhaar, PAN, Photos)
  4. Add nominee details
  5. Deposit amount via cash or cheque

9. Documents Required

  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar / PAN / Voter ID)
  • Address Proof
  • Passport-size photos
